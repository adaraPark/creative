About Me

My Name is Sandra Pelloni, I am a natural light and lifestyle phtogorapher. I am based out of Utah county. I have been photographing families, weddings, and engagements for the the past five years. I graduated in film and videography from Pennslyvania State University.

In my spare time I enjoy hiking, cooking, and reading. I love sharing my time with friends and family. I have two dogs and an amazing husband.
I would love to take phtos of your lifes memorable moments. if you think I would be a great fit for you please contact me!
